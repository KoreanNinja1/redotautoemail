Important Difference

Affiliate Invite Link vs. RedotPay App Referral

RedotPay App Referral

For regular app users who want to invite friends or individual users from inside the RedotPay App.

  • Share one referral link or referral code.
  • Best for friends and private communities.
  • Simple referral tracking inside the app.

RedotPay Affiliates Invite Link

For professional promoters who need advanced tracking, reporting, commission management, and withdrawals.

  • Create multiple promotion links.
  • Track media and placement performance.
  • Manage reports, commissions, and withdrawals in the Center.

Use Affiliates For

YouTube, Telegram, WhatsApp, Instagram, websites, paid traffic, and community campaigns.

Measure Results

Review clicks, registrations, conversions, and performance by campaign source.

Optimize Growth

Compare channels, focus on high-quality traffic, and double down on what converts.

Summary

Tier 2 Commission & Best Practices

Tier 2 Commission

You can also invite others to become RedotPay affiliates.

  • Earn 10% of their total commission.
  • The Tier 2 earning period lasts for a full 365 days.
  • Example: if your referred affiliate earns $10,000 in commission, you receive $1,000 as a Tier 2 bonus.

Best Practices

  • Create separate promotion links for different channels.
  • Use clear Media and Placement names so reports are easy to understand.
  • Track which content, platform, or community brings the best conversions.
  • Focus on quality traffic instead of only high click volume.
  • Review reports regularly and double down on the best-performing placements.
