# RedotPay Affiliates Guidebook

RedotPay Affiliates is a dedicated platform for professional promoters. It allows affiliates to create multiple invitation links, track performance across different channels, and optimize marketing efforts based on real data.

RedotPay Affiliates operates independently from the RedotPay App. Affiliate accounts, tracking data, promotion links, reports, and withdrawals are managed inside the Affiliates platform and are not automatically connected with the in-app referral system.

## 1. Affiliate Invite Link vs. RedotPay App Referral

Before using the platform, it is important to understand the difference between an Affiliate invite link and the RedotPay App referral feature.

### RedotPay App Referral

The RedotPay App referral feature is designed for regular app users who want to invite friends or individual users from inside the RedotPay App.

Typical use cases:

- Sharing one referral link or referral code from the app
- Inviting friends, followers, or private community members
- Simple referral tracking inside the RedotPay App

### RedotPay Affiliates Invite Link

The RedotPay Affiliates invite link is designed for professional promoters, KOLs, media buyers, agencies, and creators who need more advanced tracking.

Typical use cases:

- Creating multiple promotion links for different channels
- Tracking clicks, registrations, conversions, and performance by media or placement
- Comparing campaign performance across YouTube, Telegram, WhatsApp, Instagram, websites, groups, or paid traffic
- Managing affiliate commissions and withdrawal requests from the Affiliate Center

### Key Difference

In short:

- Use the **RedotPay App referral** for simple personal referrals.
- Use **RedotPay Affiliates** for professional promotion, multi-channel tracking, reporting, and commission management.

If you are a creator, KOL, or professional promoter, RedotPay Affiliates is the recommended platform.

### Which One Should You Use?

Choose **RedotPay App referral** if you only need to invite a small number of friends or community members and do not need detailed channel tracking.

Choose **RedotPay Affiliates** if you need to:

- Run campaigns across multiple channels
- Create different invite links for different media or placements
- Track clicks, registrations, and conversions separately
- Review reports and optimize future promotions
- Manage commissions and withdrawal requests in one platform

## 4. Commission Structure and Earning Details

### Card Application Fees You Can Earn From

| Card Type | Application Fee |
|---|---:|
| Virtual Card | $10 USD |
| Physical Card | $100 USD |

### Commission Levels

RedotPay Affiliates uses a level-based commission structure. Your current level is shown in the Affiliate Center.

| Level | Card Activation Commission | Consumption Commission | Second-Level Commission |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| LV1 | 20% | 0.05% | 10% |
| LV2 | 25% | 0.10% | 10% |
| LV3 | 30% | 0.20% | 10% |
| Exclusive | 40% | 0.25% | 10% |

### What This Means

Your earning potential increases as your affiliate level improves.

- Card activation commission is earned from eligible card applications.
- Consumption commission is earned from transaction volume generated by referred users.
- Second-level commission is earned from affiliates you invite to join RedotPay Affiliates.
- The final commission level and applicable rules should follow the level shown in your Affiliate Center.

## 5. Tier 2 Commission

You can also invite others to become RedotPay affiliates.

If someone joins RedotPay Affiliates through your invitation, you can earn:

- 10% of their total commission
- For a full 365 days

Example:

If your referred affiliate earns $10,000 in commission, you receive $1,000 as a Tier 2 bonus.

## 6. Best Practices for Affiliates

To improve your performance:

- Create separate promotion links for different channels.
- Use Media and Placement names clearly, so reports are easy to understand.
- Track which content, platform, or community brings the best conversions.
- Focus on quality traffic instead of only high click volume.
- Review your reports regularly and double down on the best-performing placements.
- Keep your audience informed about the correct promotion link or code.
