RedotPay Affiliates Guidebook

A practical guide for professional promoters to create invite links, track channel performance, manage commissions, and withdraw earnings through RedotPay Affiliates.

Best for: KOLs, media buyers, creators, communities, agencies, and professional promoters.

1. What Is RedotPay Affiliates?

RedotPay Affiliates is a dedicated platform for professional promoters. It allows affiliates to create multiple invitation links, track performance across different channels, and optimize marketing efforts based on real data.

RedotPay Affiliates operates independently from the RedotPay App. Affiliate accounts, tracking data, promotion links, reports, and withdrawals are managed inside the Affiliates platform and are not automatically connected with the in-app referral system.

2. Affiliate Invite Link vs. RedotPay App Referral

RedotPay App Referral

The RedotPay App referral feature is designed for regular app users who want to invite friends or individual users from inside the RedotPay App.

Best for:

  • Sharing one referral link or referral code.
  • Inviting friends, followers, or private community members.
  • Simple referral tracking inside the RedotPay App.

RedotPay Affiliates Invite Link

The RedotPay Affiliates invite link is designed for professional promoters, KOLs, media buyers, agencies, and creators who need more advanced tracking.

Best for:

  • Creating multiple promotion links for different channels.
  • Tracking clicks, registrations, conversions, and performance by media or placement.
  • Managing commissions and withdrawal requests from the Affiliates platform.
Key difference: use RedotPay App referral for simple personal referrals. Use RedotPay Affiliates for professional promotion, multi-channel tracking, reporting, and commission management.

3. How to Join RedotPay Affiliates

1Visit the Affiliate Program Page

Go to https://www.redotpay.com/affiliates, or access the Affiliate Program from the RedotPay homepage at https://www.redotpay.com/.

2Sign Up for an Affiliate Account

Fill out the required information to create your affiliate profile. You may need to provide your name, email, RedotPay ID, preferred contact method, and phone number or contact information.

3Center Overview

Once registered, you will gain access to the Center. This is where you can manage promotions, links, reports, commissions, and withdrawals.

4. How to Use RedotPay Affiliates

1Create and Share Promotion Links

Go to Promotion -> Promotion Links.

Use this feature to create unique referral links, promote different campaigns, target different audiences across multiple channels, and track where traffic is coming from.

The Media and Placement features help you categorize and track traffic sources, such as YouTube videos, Telegram groups, WhatsApp communities, Instagram stories, websites, or paid ads.

2Track Your Performance

Go to Reports.

Use Reports to view clicks, track registrations, monitor conversions, compare performance by media and placement, identify which traffic source performs best, and optimize your future promotion strategy.

3Withdraw Your Earnings

Go to Payment.

Use Payment to claim commissions from inside the RedotPay Affiliates platform, select your preferred withdrawal method, and submit a withdrawal request.

Withdrawal availability and minimum withdrawal requirements may depend on the current platform rules shown in your Center.

5. Commission Structure

Affiliates can earn from card application fees and card transaction volume.

Level Requirement Card Application Commission Card Transaction Commission
Level 1 No additional requirement. 20% of card application fees. 0.05% of card transaction volume.
Level 2 After 100 successful card application invites. 25% of card application fees. 0.25% of card transaction volume.
Level 3 Based on the level shown in your Center. 30% of card application fees. 0.20% of card transaction volume.
Level 4 Based on the level shown in your Center. 40% of card application fees. 0.25% of card transaction volume.

Important Notes

  • Your earning potential increases as your affiliate level improves.
  • Card application commission is earned from eligible card applications.
  • Card transaction commission is earned from transaction volume generated by referred users.
  • The final commission level and applicable rules should follow the level shown in your Center.

6. Tier 2 Commission

You can also invite others to become RedotPay affiliates.

If someone joins RedotPay Affiliates through your invitation, you can earn:

  • 10% of their total commission.
  • For a full 365 days.

Example: If your referred affiliate earns $10,000 in commission, you receive $1,000 as a Tier 2 bonus.

7. Best Practices for Affiliates

  • Create separate promotion links for different channels.
  • Use clear Media and Placement names, so reports are easy to understand.
  • Track which content, platform, or community brings the best conversions.
  • Focus on quality traffic instead of only high click volume.
  • Review your reports regularly and double down on the best-performing placements.
  • Keep your audience informed about the correct promotion link or code.

8. Summary

RedotPay Affiliates is best for professional promoters who need advanced tracking and commission management.

Use it to create multiple invite links, track traffic sources, compare channel performance, monitor registrations and conversions, withdraw affiliate earnings, and build long-term commission income from RedotPay card users.
