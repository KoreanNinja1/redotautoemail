## Page 1 · Quick Start + Sign Up

### RedotPay Affiliates Quick Start

Use the Affiliate Center to create promotion links, track results by channel, and manage commissions.

### Start in 3 actions

1. Open `redotpay.com/affiliates` or enter from the RedotPay homepage.
2. Register with name, email, RedotPay ID, and preferred contact method.
3. Enter the Affiliate Center and create the first promotion link.

### Keep screenshots

- Affiliate Program entry on homepage
- RedotPay Affiliates registration page

### Simple rule

- Use **Affiliates** for multi-channel links, tracking, reports, commissions, and withdrawals.
- Use **App referral** only for simple personal invites.

## Page 3 · Reports + Payment

### Weekly operating checklist

1. Create one promotion link per channel or campaign.
2. Use clear **Media** and **Placement** names.
3. Review **Reports** weekly: clicks, registrations, conversions, and performance by placement.
4. Double down on placements that bring real conversions, not only high clicks.
5. Go to **Payment** to claim commissions and submit withdrawal requests.
