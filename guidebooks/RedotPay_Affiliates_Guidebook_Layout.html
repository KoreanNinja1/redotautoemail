Commission

Commission Structure

You can earn from card application fees and card transaction volume.

Level Card Application Commission Card Transaction Commission
LV1 20% of card application fees. 0.05% of card transaction volume.
LV2 40% of card application fees. 0.25% of card transaction volume.

Note: the affiliate program currently only uses LV1 and LV2. LV1 follows the original LV1 benefits, and LV2 follows the original LV4 benefits. The original LV2 and LV3 levels are no longer used.

Important Notes

  • Card application commission comes from eligible card applications.
  • Card transaction commission comes from referred users' card transaction volume.
  • Final rules should follow the level shown in your Center.

Tier 2 Commission

If someone joins RedotPay Affiliates through your invitation, you can earn 10% of their total commission for 365 days.

Example: if they earn $10,000 in commission, you receive $1,000.
