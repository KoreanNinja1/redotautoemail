## Page 3 · 入驻流程 · 付费合作 · 联系

### How to onboard *(free card & referral setup)*

1. Register a RedotPay account.
2. Complete KYC in the app.
3. Send your **RedotPay ID** for confirmation.
4. After confirmation — **free card** to test the product.
5. We set up your **referral link, code, and custom discount code**.
6. Create content in your style; include your referral info.
7. We track activations, spending, and campaign performance.

### Paid collaboration

We are open to paid deals, but **new partners usually start with a trial** first.

Long-term contracts are mainly for KOLs we have worked with before. For new partners: test performance in **2–4 weeks** after the first video or private-group post. Strong results → long-term contract or upfront budget discussion.

Same approach for larger creators (including 2–3M followers): test → review data → decide budget.
